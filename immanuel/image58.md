## Translation

1. Louise Rosete [10/14/98] 
born December 19, 1858, baptized January 23, 1868.
Parents:
  - Fridrich Schönstedt
  - Agnese née Mitteldorp.
  Witnesses: Jakob Stöhr and his wife Elisabeth.
2. Rufus Chancey [3/10/96]
born December 3, 1863, baptized January 23, 1868.
Parents:
  - Fridrich Schönstedt
  - Agnese née Mitteldorp.
  Godparents: Jakob Stöhr and his wife Elisabeth.
3. Franziska Emeline
[to Ill. 1890Kirsck]
born November 20, 1865, died June 28, 1868
Parents:
  - Friedrich Schönstedt
  - Agnese née Mitteldorp.
  Godparents: Jakob Stöhr and his wife Elisabeth.
4. Sophie Christine Wilhelmine [+5/9/95]
born on January 25, 1868, baptized on February 2, 1866.
Parents:
  - Christian Dietrich Schamerloh
  - Caroline Philippine Sophie née Krückeberg
  Godparents: Mrs. Caroline Heckmann and Mrs. Wilhelmine Weiland.
5. Heinrich Theodor
born on February 13, 2 a.m., born on February 21, 1868.
Parents:
  - Johann Andreas Thieme
  - Christine Sophie Eleoncze née Krückeberg.
  Godparents: Gottfried Thieme and Carl Grote
6. Maria Isabella
born on February 17 and baptized on February 23, 1868.
[RungerSt. Louis]
Parents:
  - Carl Fridrigs Wilhelm Blocke
  - Maria née Bibelheimer
  Godparents: Virgin Maria Blecke and Virgin Isabella Bibelheimer.
