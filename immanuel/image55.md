## Translation

1. Maria Catharina Elisabeth
born on September 30, baptized on November 10, 1865.
Parents:
  - Conrad Johann Heinrich Fells.
  - Caroline née Feldner.
  Godparents: Catharina Blum and Elisabeth Rücklos.
2. Joseph Anton
born on November 26, 1865, baptized on January 9, 1866
Parents:
  - Adam Höriger
  - Charlotte Anna née Braun
  Godparents: Jakob Friedrich Höriger and his wife Maria.
3. Allise Allass.
born on November 29, 1865, baptized on January 9, 1866.
Parents:
  - Levi Baron
  - Wilhelmine née Höriger.
  Godparents: Jakob Fridrich Höriger and his wife Maria.
4. Fridrich Christian
Parents:
  - Christian Dietrich Schamerloh
  - Caroline Philippine Sophie née Krückeberg
  Godparents: Carl Grote
born on January 17, baptized on January 18, 1866.
5. Louise Maria
born on January 22 and baptized on January 28, 1866
Parents:
  - Carl Heinrich Ludwig Koldeweÿ
  - Louise Sophie née Winkelmann.
  Godparents: Louise Wittfeld and Maria Tiele.
6. Theodor Emilius
born on February 12 and baptized on February 25, 1866
Parents:
  - Jakob Heinrich Rücklos
  - Anna Elisabeth née Hoffmann
  Godparents: Abraham Stendler and Heinrich Rücklos.
