## Translation

1. Johanna Martha Maria
born August 16, 1874, baptized August 23, 1874.
Parents:
  - Heinrich Rücklos
  - Friederick, née
    Godparents: Mrs. Johanna Ebel and Mrs. Hoier
    1. Caemmerer, Pastor.
2. Henriette Christine Mathilde [Ferd. Bleeke]
born November 19, 1874, died November 29, 1874
Parents:
  - Ludwig Koldewey
  - his wife Sophia.
  Godparents:
  1. H. Caemmerer, Pastor
3. Hermmann Wilhelm Ferdinand [+3/1/49]
Son of:
  - Ferdinand and Maria Reinking
born January 21, 1875, baptized January 31, 1875.
  Godparents: Herrmann Reinking, Wilhelm Bleeke, and Heinrich Bleeke
  1. H. Caemmerer
4. Sophia Emilia
Daughter of:
  - Friedrich Roth and Theresa, née Büttner
born February 7, 1875, baptized February 15, 1875
  Godparents: Doris Haugk and Cristine Büttner
  1. H. Caemmerer, Pastor
5. Hermann, Heinrich Wilhelm [+1940]
Son of:
  - Carl Grote and Caroline, née Krückeberg
born April 7, 1875, baptized April 11, 1865.
  Godparents: Heinrich Krückeberg and W. Reinking. W. Grote,
Chr. Schemerloh, Vice-Councilor
  1. H. Caemmerer, Pastor
6. Johann Christian Ferdinand Hugo [Detroit?]
Son of:
  - Aug. Hugo Caemmerer and Margaretha, née Beck
born March 10, 1876, baptized March 12
  Godparents: Johann Ebel, Christian Bleeke, and Ferdinand Reinking.
  1. H. Caemmerer, Pastor
