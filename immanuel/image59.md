## Translation

1. [+] Conrad Christian Wilhelm
born on February 29th at 11 a.m., baptized on March 1st, 1868.
Parents:
  - Christian Wilhelm Fells.
  - Elisabeth née Rup
  Godparents: Christian Bleeke and Conrad Fells.
2. [+4/3/18] Caroline Wilhelmine Bernhardine
born on May 2nd, 1868, baptized on May 10th, 1868
Parents:
  - Carl Heinrich Ludwig Koldewey
  - Louise Sophie née Winkelmann
  Godparents: Bernhardine Koldewey and Wilhelmine Schamerloh
3. [+1/14/00] Martin Andreas Wilhelm
born on March 3rd at 1:30 a.m., baptized on May 10th, 1868
Parents:
  - Conrad Dietrich Ferdinand Reinking
  - Maria Louise Eleonore née Bleeke
  Godparents: Wilhelm Heckmann, Andreas Thieme, and Wilhelm Reinking
4. Sahra Isabella
born April 10, 1868, baptized October 4, 1868
Parents:
  - Levi Baron
  - Wilhelmine, née Höreger.
  Godparents: Jacob Fridrich Höreger and his wife Maria.
5. Sahra Catharine Sophie
born October 13, 1868, and baptized October 25, 1868
Parents:
  - Heinrich Jakob Rücklos
  - Friderike, née Dauer.
  Godparents: Virgin Sahra Belz and Virgin Sophie Bleeke.
6. [no name is present]
born October 17, 1868, and baptized November 12, 1868
Parents:
  - Georg Blum
  - Catharina, née Feldner
  Godparents: Conrad Fells and Jakob Stöhr
