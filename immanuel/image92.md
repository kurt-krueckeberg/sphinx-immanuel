## Translation

1. Otto Christian Andreas [+]
born February 28, 1891, baptized March 8, 1891.
Parents:
  - Theodore Thieme
  - Karolina, née Schamerloh
  Godparents: Christ[ian] Schamerloh, Ab. Steudler
H. Schmidt
2. Herbert Friedrich Gottfried [Ft. W.]
born June 3, 1891, baptized June 14, 1891
Parents:
  - Eduard Bleeke
  - Pauline, née Thieme
  Godparents: Friedrich Blecke Sr., Gottfried Thieme
H. Schmidt
3. Gotthilf. [+9/3/91]
born September 3 1871. Baptism was given in urgency. Died on September 3, 1891
Parents:
  - Ch. Marbach
  - Karoline née [Deuring?]
4. Arnold Wilhelm Clemens [Ft.W.]
born on November 11, 1891. Baptized on November 15, 1891
Parents:
  - Mrs. Bienz
  - Helene née Reinking
  Parent: Clamor Fülling, Adam Bienz
H. Schmidt
5. Mathilde Karoline Louise [Jacob Trim]
born on September 18, 1891. Baptized on September 21, 1871 by Sophie Schamerloh
in the presence of her mother.
Parents:
  - H. Railing
  - Auguste née Schamerloh
  Godparents: Mrs. Hy. Krückeberg & Mrs. W. Weyland
6. Clarence Heinrich Friedrich
born November 24, 1891. Buried in the church
Parents:
  - Rufus Schönstedt
  - El. née Kirkland
  Godparents: H. Grote
