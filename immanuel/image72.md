  - Heinrich Kreckeberg
  - Louise née Buuck
Godparents: 1) Mrs. Sophie Buuk and 2) Mrs. Caroline Krückeberg
2. Amalie Christine, [+]

  - Hermann Büttner

born on October 10th and baptized on October 17th, 1880

  Godparents: Mrs. Louise Büttner and Mrs. Emilie [Steinmann/Neiemann]

born on October 26th and baptized on November 1881 [?] (1880
Parents:
  - Fridrich Roth
  - Therese née Büttner
  Godparents: 1) Fridrich Thieme, 2) Gottlob Büttner, 3) Hermann Büttner.
