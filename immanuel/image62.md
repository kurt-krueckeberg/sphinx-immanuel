## Translation

1. Anna Pauline Elisabeth [+]
born on January 18th and baptized on February 1st, 1870.
Parents:
  - Carl Fridrich Martin Scharbade.
  - Christine Friderike née Meile
  Godparents: Mrs. Maria Bleeke and Maid: Sara, Belz.
2. Scharlotta Paulina. [Nebr.]
born on October 27th and baptized on November 6th, 1869.
Parents:
  - Andreas Thieme
  - Sophia née Klein
  Godparents: Dares Haugh and Charlotte Rupprecht
3. Carl Friedrich Martin
born on May 16th and baptized on May 22nd, 1870.
Parents:
  - Carl Grote
  - Caroline née Krückeberg
  Godparents: Andreas Thieme and Christian Kohlmeyer.
4. Maria Elisabeth
born on April 18th and baptized on June 19th, 1870.
Parents:
  - Johannes Hoeuzer
  - Elisabeth, née Sallmann
  Godparents: Jacob Friedrich Haeriger and his wife Maria.
5. Sophia Louise Amanda [Rev. JaelkIowa]
born on July 20th and baptized on July 31st, 1870.
Parents:
  - Friedrich Bleeke
  - Maria, née Bibilhamer.
  Godparents: Sophia Blecke, Louise Reinking, and Amanda Meier.
6. Karolina Louisa Sophia
born on September 8th and died on September 18th, 1870.
Parents:
  - Christian Schamerloh
  - Karolina, née Krükeberg
  Godparents: Lauisa Reinking and Sophia Grote.
