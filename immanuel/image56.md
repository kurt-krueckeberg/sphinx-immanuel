## Translation

1. Conrad Christian Wilhelm
born on February 25th and baptized on March 4th, 1866.
Parents:
  - Christian Wilhelm Fells
  - Elisabeth née Rup.
  Godparents: Christine Sophie Eleonore Thieme and Sara Bup.
2. Auguste Maria Johanne [+]
born on August 28th and baptized on September 2nd, 1866.
Parents:
  - Heinrich Steger
  - Maria Elise née Stoppenhagen.
  Godparents: Auguste Stoppenhagen, Maria Bleeke and Johanne Steger.
3. Ferdinand Carl Fridrich. [+]
born on November 11th and baptized on November 25th, 1866
Parents:
  - Conrad Dietrich Ferdinand Reinking
  - Maria Louise Eleonore née Bleeke
    Godparents: Fridrich Reinking, Carl Heckmann and Fridrich Bleeke. Emma Carolina
born on November 16th and baptized on December 2nd, 1866
Parents:
  - Heinrich Jakob Rücklos
  - Friderike née Dauer.
  Godparents: Emeline Rabenalt and Caroline Schamerloh.
4. Hermann Fridrich Wilhelm [+8/21/09]
born on November 24th and baptized on November 26th, 1866.
Parents:
  - Conrad Christian Wilhelm Reinking.
  - Ernestine Bertha née Busch.
Godparents: Fridrich Busch and Georg Conrad Wilhelm Reinking.
5. Abraham Heinrich
born on September 16th, 1866, baptized on January 1st, 1867.
Parents:
  - Michael Bräuner
  - Johanna née Belz.
  Godparents: Abraham Steudler and his wife Maria.
